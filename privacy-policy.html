Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 11, 2026

PDF Compressor ("we", "our", or "the App") is a PDF compression and image-to-PDF conversion tool. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use the App.

Information We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect, store, or share:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number, etc.)
  • Location data or device identifiers
  • Any files or documents you process with the App

How Your Files Are Processed

All PDF and image files you select are processed entirely on your device. No file content is ever uploaded to any server or transmitted over the internet. Compressed and converted output files are saved locally to your device storage.

Permissions

The App requests access to your device file storage solely to:

  • Allow you to select PDF and image files
  • Save compressed and converted output files

Third-Party Services

This App integrates the following third-party services:

  • Google AdMob — Displays banner and interstitial advertisements within the App. AdMob may collect device advertising identifiers and usage data for ad personalization, subject to Google Privacy Policy.
  • Google Play In-App Purchases — Processes Pro and Premium upgrade transactions. Payment processing is handled entirely by Google Play.

Users can opt out of ad personalization via their device settings (Settings → Google → Ads).

Children Privacy

This App is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be effective immediately upon posting.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: pdfcompressor.app@gmail.com
